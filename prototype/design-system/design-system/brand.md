# Brand DNA — feel, shape, color treatment, imagery, GAIN

> Load for brand-expression calls: visual direction, surface composition, illustration, mascot/Ed moments.
> Solve Education! Mission: turn learning into livelihood through behavioural science.

## BRD · Brand feel
Human · optimistic · curved/active · *looks like learning*. Never corporate, sterile, or hostile.
Audience: learners 16–24, Indonesia-first, low-tier Android. Emotional thread: **Mobility · Opportunity · Dignity**.

## BRD · GAIN (product pillars — always capitalize GAIN)
- **G — Gamification:** points, challenges, leaderboards. Celebrate every small win; progress is visible, not hidden.
- **A — AI Coach ("Ed"):** instant personalized feedback; adapts to skill, pace, interest.
- **I — Incentives:** concrete rewards tied to visible milestones (data vouchers, workshops, merch).
- **N — Network:** peers + partners → belonging and accountability. Isolation is the enemy of persistence.
- **BRD-01** Explain GAIN in plain terms; never imply guaranteed outcomes or "proprietary magic".

## BRD · Shape language (see NN-10)
- **BRD-02** Prefer soft curves, arcs, semi-circles, organic blobs — forms that echo the logo's motion.
- **BRD-03** Avoid sharp/boxy/hostile geometry as the dominant language.
- **BRD-04** Dense admin/sponsor surfaces may be structured, but preserve warmth via spacing, copy, color, softened components.

## BRD · Color treatment (see NN-09)
- **BRD-05** One dominant color per surface + neutral support. Solid fills over heavy gradients (subtle gradients only when they support the direction).
- **BRD-06** Generous whitespace (or brand-yellow space) for clarity; some asymmetry OK when it strengthens the story; stack vertically when unsure; don't over-compress.
- **BRD-07** Maintain WCAG AA. Don't stack many bright colors or slip into muted corporate palettes.

## BRD · Imagery & illustration
- **BRD-08** Real learners + candid moments over staged stock; show progression. Learner imagery: 15–25, balanced gender, ID/MY/SG focus.
- **BRD-09** Illustrations rounded, optimistic, learning-oriented. Icons rounded line/solid — not sharp enclosed badges by default.
- **BRD-10** No approved asset? Describe the intended image and mark it clearly as an unvalidated placeholder.

## BRD · Approval filter (revise if any fail)
- [ ] Feels human
- [ ] Feels optimistic
- [ ] Looks like learning, not generic corporate success
- [ ] Reflects curved / active / human brand qualities
- [ ] Public-facing: contains hook + proof + system

## BRD · Logo (hard constraints)
- **BRD-11** Use the full official logo only; never modify its color, proportions, or effects. On busy backgrounds, place it on a white/neutral plate.
