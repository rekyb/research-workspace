# Platform Merdeka Mengajar / Rumah Pendidikan (Ruang GTK): observations & patterns

**Role in this study:** the **Indonesian teacher edtech** anchor, the most context-relevant
platform. Capture is limited to the public Play listing (in-app flow is SSO-gated).

## What stood out
- **Platform restructuring (important context).** The old **Platform Merdeka Mengajar (PMM)**
  → **Ruang GTK** → now **"Rumah Pendidikan (Ruang GTK)"** (updated Jul 2026). Any product
  reference to "PMM" should note it now lives inside Rumah Pendidikan.
  *Evidence: `screenshots/01-play-listing.png` + listing description text.*
- **Government scale.** 4.9★, **228K reviews, 5M+ downloads**, Government publisher. This is
  the dominant, state-backed teacher platform, so its incentive model (official certificates
  tied to PD credit) sets the reference point for what Indonesian teachers expect.
- **Certificate flow is login-gated.** The *Sertifikat* from *Pelatihan Mandiri* requires
  belajar.id SSO; not captured (guardrails). Documented as a gate, not a finding.

## Pattern worth synthesizing (from literature + context, not from captured UI)
- **In Indonesia the certificate is a credential-signal.** The certification literature is
  directly Indonesian: the 2007-08 program raised status/pay without improving teaching
  (Kusumawardhani 2017; de Ree et al. 2018), i.e. teachers value the certificate as an
  external signal tied to PD credit and civil-service advancement, not as competence feedback.
- **But enjoyment drives continued use of PMM.** Hedonic motivation (curiosity, joy) predicts
  teachers' intention to keep using PMM (UNNES HMSAM study; UTAUT2 n=383). Implication: the
  *certificate* supplies the extrinsic reason to finish; *enjoyable* in-app mechanics supply
  the reason to keep coming back. This is the core cert-plus-simple-gamification thesis, and it
  is the one place we have direct Indonesian evidence for it. See `references.md`.

## Gaps / caveats
- **No in-app capture.** The certificate presentation, any badges/points, and the training
  flow are behind government SSO and were not accessed. All PMM-specific *effect* claims rest
  on the literature, not on captured UI. Cross-context transfer of the Western badge evidence
  to this platform remains a hypothesis to validate locally.
- Play reviews (public) were read for context but **not** screenshotted, to avoid committing
  third-party reviewer names.
