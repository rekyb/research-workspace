# External Domain References (`/review-research` Debate)
**Study:** `2026-07-16-indonesian-teacher-onboarding-literature`
**Recorded date:** 2026-07-16

These external authoritative domain sources were introduced during the multi-persona peer-review debate (`Seat 2 Domain Expert` & `Seat 3 Evidence Auditor`) to ground institutional, civil service, and technical mobile realities in Indonesian educational governance:

1. **Kemendikdasmen Pusat Informasi Belajar.id & Rumah Pendidikan (2024–2026)**
   - **Topic:** Technical troubleshooting, Google Workspace 2-Step Verification (2SV/2FA) lockouts, Dapodik synchronization delays, and SSO hurdles for `Guru Honorer` vs. `PNS/PPPK`.
   - **URL / Portal:** `https://pusatinformasi.guru.kemdikbud.go.id/`
   - **Relevance to Study:** Grounds the finding that mandatory upfront `belajar.id` SSO creates severe access friction on low-end Android handsets (`Feature 1`).

2. **Kemendikdasmen & BKN Integrasi e-Kinerja & Panduan Pengelolaan Kinerja Guru (2024–2026)**
   - **Topic:** Integration between Platform Merdeka Mengajar (PMM) and BKN Civil Service Performance System (`e-Kinerja`), *Bukti Karya* (Evidence of Work), and semester performance appraisals (`Kepala Sekolah` reviews).
   - **URL / Portal:** `https://pusatinformasi.guru.kemdikbud.go.id/` & `https://ekinerja.bkn.go.id/`
   - **Relevance to Study:** Explains why teachers exhibit administrative burnout (*Aksi Nyata* fatigue) and why practical outputs like `Modul Ajar / RPP` hold true psychological ownership (`Feature 2`).

3. **Perdirjen GTK No. 7607/B.B1/HK.03/2023** (15 Dec 2023)
   - **Topic:** *Pedoman Pengelolaan Kinerja Guru* — RHK / `e-Kinerja` mechanics and the 32-point-per-semester *Pengembangan Kompetensi* requirement.
   - **URL:** `https://guru.kemendikdasmen.go.id/dokumen/Av5DVbD2Jq`
   - **Relevance to Study:** Grounds the finding that standalone unaccredited app PDF certificates carry zero official *Angka Kredit* valuation (`Feature 3`). Credit accrues through the teacher's own RHK entry approved by the `Kepala Sekolah`.
   - **Correction (2026-07-17):** Supersedes the earlier grounding on **PermenPANRB No. 6/2022**, which was misapplied — that regulation *decoupled* functional-position kinerja from the old butir-kegiatan / angka-kredit system and does not establish the zero-credit finding. Whether app-mediated **MGMP/KKG 32 JP** activity can carry `e-Kinerja BKN` recognition is an unverified governance hypothesis (see `SYNTHESIS.md` › `Gaps & caveats`), not an established requirement.

4. **KepmenPANRB No. 16 Tahun 2025 — Dapodik entry lock for new `Guru Honorer`**
   - **Topic:** New honorary teachers entering service after 31 December 2024 can no longer be registered in Dapodik.
   - **URLs:** Kompas (2026-05-11) `https://www.kompas.com/edu/read/2026/05/11/203157771/`; Tirto `https://tirto.id/berapa-lama-guru-honorer-bisa-masuk-dapodik-gQRx`
   - **Relevance to Study:** Structural access exclusion for a growing `Guru Honorer` segment (no Dapodik → no `belajar.id`); the regulation-grounded anchor for deferred / independent registration (`Feature 1`).

5. **Litovsky, S., Loewenstein, G., Horn, S., & Olivola, C. Y. (2022)** — corrected endowment-effect citation
   - *PNAS* 119(34):e2202700119, DOI `10.1073/pnas.2202700119`; `https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35972966/`
   - **Relevance to Study:** Correct source for the endowment / loss-vs-gain framing *direction* (`Feature 2`); replaces the prior "Charpentier et al." miscitation logged in `sources.md`.

6. **Loss-aversion magnitude literature** (contextualizing $\lambda$)
   - Brown, Imai, Vieider & Camerer (2024), meta-analysis of 607 estimates, $\lambda \approx 1.955$ with large heterogeneity: `https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0167487024000485`. A 2025 re-meta-analysis ("Loss aversion is not robust") contests it: `https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0167487025000133`.
   - **Relevance to Study:** Establishes that $\lambda \approx 2.2$ is contested and not a stable transferable constant; use loss-framing as a *direction*, not a magnitude (`Feature 2`).
