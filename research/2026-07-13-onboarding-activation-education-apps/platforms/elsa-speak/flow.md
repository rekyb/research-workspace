## Steps (observable, logged-out)

1. **Landing / value framing (localized) + paywall.** `elsaspeak.com` geo-redirects to `id.elsaspeak.com`. Hero: "Penilaian keterampilan Berbicara Bebas" (Free speaking-skill assessment) with a "Tingkatkan sekarang" (Improve now) CTA — and a large **Flash Sale** promo (ELSA Premium lifetime 80% off, Rp1,666,000). The whole site is in Indonesian. → `01-landing-localized-value-framing-paywall.png`

2. **"English test" page — app onboarding shown as mockups.** The "Tes bahasa Inggris" nav leads to `/english-test/`: "Tingkatkan Segera Kepercayaan Dirimu Saat Komunikasi Bahasa Inggris" with phone **mockups of the actual app onboarding UI** — a coach picker ("Pelatih / Latihan Hari Ini rekomendasi ELSA"), a "Terekomendasi" (Recommended) personalized lesson with a MULAI (Start) button, IELTS vs "Real-life Communication" paths, and a **score/level result** mockup ("LEVEL ANDA: Advanced 84%"). This is how Elsa depicts its assessment-led, personalized onboarding — evidence of the app flow we can't interact with. → `02-english-test-app-mockups.png`

3. **Real web speaking assessment (the interactive part).** Below the fold, a working widget: a **sentence to pronounce** ("This summer I will visit a new country with two of my best friends."), an **audio-playback icon** (hear a model reading), and a large **microphone record button**. Feature claims above it (Indonesian): scored against a "Standar Native," syllable-level error detection, "pembelajaran yang terpersonalisasi berdasarkan kemampuanmu" (personalized learning based on your ability). → `03-web-speaking-assessment.png`

4. **Mic interaction — record state, no priming.** Clicking the mic **immediately begins recording** (button turns blue → **green** with a listening state); **no rationale/priming screen precedes it** (the browser permission was already granted in-profile, so no dialog appeared this session). Clear recording-state feedback via color change. Clicking again stops it. → `04-mic-recording-active.png`

5. **[Gated] Full app onboarding + scored result + personalized plan.** The complete flow — coach selection, level placement, a scored pronunciation report, and the personalized daily plan — happens in the native app (or after the web test completes into a lead-capture/app-download prompt). Not fully captured; the app-store listing would show more.

## Where friction / delight / dead-ends appear
- **Delight / smart hook:** embedding a *real, working* speaking test in the marketing page is an excellent low-commitment taste — you experience the core value (speak → get scored) before any signup or download.
- **Localization (major strength for our audience):** the entire experience is in Indonesian, our learners' language — the single most audience-relevant find of the five.
- **Friction / dead-ends:** the mic fires with **no priming** (contrast Duolingo) — on a real device (not this pre-granted profile) that raw prompt is exactly the device-permission dead-end our own learners hit. And a **subscription paywall** dominates the site.
- **App-first gap:** the true onboarding is app-only; web is a funnel to download/subscribe.
