# CodeSignal (Learn) — Onboarding Flow (desktop web)

**Surface:** desktop web (`codesignal.com/learn`), captured 2026-07-13 (logged-out). Desktop-labeled.
**Entry point → goal:** learning-paths catalogue → "Start learning" → **immediate account-creation wall** → (skill assessment + personalized path, gated).
**One-line summary:** CodeSignal Learn puts a **full account-creation form up front** — clicking "Start learning" from the catalogue redirects straight to a name + email + password signup (with social options); its signature **AI-assessment-led placement (via "Cosmo")** sits entirely behind that wall, so it was not captured (we do not create accounts).

**Capture scope note:** by agreement (2026-07-13) we documented the gate rather than creating an account, since two strong advanced-routing exemplars (Duolingo, Brilliant) were already fully captured. Evidence is the logged-out surface: `flow.gif` (signup gate), `catalogue.gif` (paths); stills in `screenshots/`.

---

## Steps (observable, logged-out)

1. **Catalogue / value framing.** `codesignal.com/learn/course-paths` — "Learning paths / Turn screen time into skills time. Chat with Cosmo, build real skills…" (Cosmo is CodeSignal's AI tutor, shown as an astronaut mascot). A hero learning-path card plus **topic Collections** — Generative AI, Business & Leadership, Interview Prep, AI & Machine Learning, Learn to Code, Data Science & Engineering — and a Trending row. Browsable without an account; organized by **topic collection** (contrast Khan's grade organization). → `03-catalogue-collections.png`

2. **"Start learning" → account wall.** The primary CTA redirects to `identity.codesignal.com/auth/signup`: "**Create your account**" — a full form of **First name, Last name, Email, Password**, a Privacy/ToS agreement checkbox, and a **Get started** button. → `01-signup-gate-create-account.png`

3. **Social sign-up options.** Below the form: **Continue with Single Sign-On, Apple, Google, Facebook**. So a one-tap path exists, but it still creates an account. → `02-signup-social-options.png`

4. **[Gated] Skill assessment + personalized path.** CodeSignal Learn's distinctive onboarding — an AI-driven skill assessment (with Cosmo) that places the learner into the right point of a path — sits beyond the account wall and was **not captured** (no account created). This is the platform's advanced-routing mechanic and the reason it was selected; it is documented here as gated, not observed.

---

## Where friction / delight / dead-ends appear
- **Friction (dominant):** the heaviest upfront wall of the five — a full name + email + password form is demanded before *any* content or assessment. Even Khan only asks role + age before its (also mandatory) account step; CodeSignal front-loads the whole form.
- **Note:** the catalogue's topic-collection organization is clear and browsable, but "Start learning" gives no try-before-signup taste at all.
- **Uncaptured value:** the assessment-led placement (Cosmo) is likely CodeSignal's strongest onboarding asset for the advanced-user lens, but it is unobservable without an account.
